6-Day Kenya Safari: Amboseli, L. Naivasha & Maasai Mara

DURATION 6 Days / 5 Nights
TOUR TYPE Safari
DESTINATIONS Amboseli • L. Naivasha • Maasai Mara

Safari Overview

Embark on an unforgettable 6-day journey through Kenya's most iconic wildlife destinations. From the majestic elephants of Amboseli with stunning views of Mount Kilimanjaro, to the serene waters of Lake Naivasha, and finally the world-renowned Maasai Mara National Reserve – this safari offers the perfect blend of wildlife viewing, scenic beauty, and authentic African adventure.

📍 Destinations: Amboseli National Park, Lake Naivasha, Maasai Mara National Reserve
🦁 Safari Focus: Big Five, Elephant Herds, Birdlife, Great Migration (July-Oct)

Why Choose This Safari?

This 6-day safari combines Kenya's most famous wildlife destinations in a perfectly paced itinerary. Amboseli offers unmatched elephant viewing with the dramatic backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro. Lake Naivasha provides a serene contrast with its boat safaris and walking opportunities. The Maasai Mara needs no introduction – it's simply one of the world's greatest wildlife spectacles, especially during the Great Migration season.

Best Time to Visit

This safari is excellent year-round, but the Great Migration in Maasai Mara (July to October) offers the most dramatic wildlife viewing. The dry seasons (January-February and June-October) provide the best game viewing conditions overall.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Nairobi – Amboseli National Park

Meals: Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Tulia Amboseli Lodge

Upon arrival at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA), you will be met by our representative and briefed about your safari. Drive south across the Athi plains towards Amboseli National Park, arriving at Tulia Amboseli Lodge in time for lunch and check-in.

In the afternoon, enjoy your first game drive in the park, well known for its large elephant herds and spectacular views of Mount Kilimanjaro. Watch the sun set behind Africa's highest peak before returning to the lodge for dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Amboseli – Full Day Game Drive

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Tulia Amboseli Lodge

After breakfast, head out for a full-day game drive with a packed picnic lunch. Spend the day exploring Amboseli's varied habitats, from swamps and acacia woodlands to open plains, while spotting elephants, buffalo, lions, cheetahs, and a wide range of bird species.

The park's unique ecosystem creates excellent wildlife viewing opportunities throughout the day. In the evening, return to Tulia Amboseli Lodge for dinner and overnight.

Day 3: Amboseli – Great Rift Valley (Naivasha)

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Great Rift Valley Lodge & Golf Resort

After breakfast, check out and drive towards the Great Rift Valley, arriving at Great Rift Valley Lodge & Golf Resort in time for lunch.

In the afternoon, enjoy a scenic boat ride on Lake Naivasha, where you may spot hippos and over 400 species of birdlife, followed by a guided nature walk on Crescent Island Sanctuary, home to giraffes, zebras, wildebeest, and various antelope species. This is a unique opportunity to walk among wildlife in a safe, predator-free environment. Return to the lodge for dinner and overnight.

Day 4: Naivasha – Maasai Mara National Reserve

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Zebra Plains Mara Camp

After an early breakfast, depart Naivasha and drive through the Rift Valley escarpments to the world-famous Maasai Mara National Reserve. Arrive at Zebra Plains Mara Camp in time for lunch and check-in.

In the afternoon, enjoy your first game drive in the Mara, where vast savannahs host an incredible diversity of wildlife including lions, leopards, cheetahs, elephants, and numerous plains game. Return to camp for dinner and overnight.

Day 5: Maasai Mara – Full Day Game Drive

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Zebra Plains Mara Camp

After an early breakfast, set off for a full-day game drive with a packed picnic lunch. Explore the rolling grasslands and riverine forests of the Mara, searching for the Big Five, cheetahs, hyenas, and other predators.

Between July and October, you may witness the Great Migration, one of nature's most dramatic spectacles, as millions of wildebeest and zebras cross the Mara River. Your expert guide will position you for the best wildlife viewing and photography opportunities. Return to Zebra Plains Mara Camp in the evening for dinner and overnight.

Day 6: Maasai Mara – Nairobi / Departure

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: N/A

After breakfast, check out and enjoy a game drive en route as you leave the Maasai Mara. Drive back to Nairobi, arriving in the afternoon.

You will be transferred to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) for your outbound international flight, marking the end of your safari adventure with unforgettable memories of Kenya's wildlife.
