Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Maldives

Meals: Dinner | Overnight: Resort Villa

Arrive at Velana International Airport in Malé. Meet your resort representative who will assist with your transfer to the resort via speedboat or seaplane (depending on resort location). Enjoy the scenic journey across the turquoise waters. Upon arrival, receive a warm welcome with tropical drinks and cold towels. Check into your luxury villa decorated with honeymoon amenities. Evening at leisure to explore the resort facilities and enjoy a romantic dinner.

Day 2: Beach Relaxation & Snorkeling

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Resort Villa

Wake up to a beautiful sunrise and enjoy breakfast with ocean views. Spend the morning relaxing on your private deck or the pristine beach. After lunch, explore the house reef with complimentary snorkeling equipment. Discover colorful coral gardens, tropical fish, sea turtles, and possibly reef sharks in crystal-clear waters. In the evening, take a romantic walk along the beach and enjoy sunset cocktails followed by dinner at the resort's main restaurant.

Day 3: Sunset Dolphin Cruise & Spa

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Resort Villa

After breakfast, enjoy a leisurely morning by the pool or beach. Indulge in a rejuvenating 60-minute couple's spa treatment at the resort's wellness center. In the afternoon, embark on a romantic sunset dolphin cruise. Watch spinner dolphins playing in their natural habitat while sipping champagne as the sun sets over the Indian Ocean. Return to the resort for a special candlelit beach dinner under the stars, with your feet in the sand and waves as your soundtrack.

Day 4: Water Sports & Island Activities

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Resort Villa

Enjoy an active day trying various water sports activities. Try kayaking, stand-up paddleboarding, windsurfing, or catamaran sailing (non-motorized water sports included). For adventure enthusiasts, optional activities like jet skiing, parasailing, or scuba diving are available at extra cost. Afternoon at leisure to relax at the spa, swim in the pool, or simply lounge in your villa. Evening dinner at one of the resort's specialty restaurants.

Optional: PADI diving course or certified diving trips available

Day 5: Free Day - Leisure or Optional Excursions

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Resort Villa

Today is yours to design as you wish. Relax and enjoy the resort facilities, take a morning yoga class, or book optional excursions. Popular options include island hopping to visit local villages, fishing trips, seaplane scenic tours over the atolls, or diving at world-famous sites. For a truly unique experience, book the underwater restaurant dining (at select resorts) or a private sandbank picnic. Enjoy quality time together in paradise.

Day 6: Last Full Day in Paradise

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Resort Villa

Make the most of your final full day in the Maldives. Enjoy a lazy morning breakfast in your villa or beachside. Take final photos of the stunning scenery, collect shells from the beach, or simply soak in the infinity pool. Enjoy your last snorkeling session at the house reef or book a final spa treatment. In the evening, savor a farewell dinner and perhaps join evening entertainment or simply stargaze from your deck, creating lasting memories of your honeymoon.

Day 7: Departure

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: N/A

Enjoy a final breakfast at the resort. Depending on your flight time, you may have a few more hours to relax before check-out. Transfer by speedboat or seaplane back to Velana International Airport. If time permits, explore Malé city for some last-minute souvenir shopping. Depart for your flight back to Nairobi with unforgettable memories of your Maldives honeymoon.

Optional: Late checkout available at extra cost (subject to availability)

Flight Duration: Approximately 11-13 hours (with layover)

Turkish Airlines via Istanbul

Route: Nairobi (NBO) → Istanbul (IST) → Malé (MLE)

Flight Duration: Approximately 12-14 hours (with layover)

Frequency: Several flights per week

Good option with comfortable transit and good service.

SriLankan Airlines via Colombo

Route: Nairobi (NBO) → Colombo (CMB) → Malé (MLE)

Flight Duration: Approximately 10-11 hours (with layover)

Frequency: Regular flights

Direct routing via Colombo with shorter total travel time.

Baggage Allowance

  • Economy Class: 30kg checked baggage + 7-10kg hand luggage
  • Business Class: 40kg checked baggage + 7-10kg hand luggage
  • Extra baggage can be purchased in advance at discounted rates

Resort Transfer Information

  • Speedboat Transfer: 20-45 minutes (for nearby resorts)
  • Seaplane Transfer: 20-45 minutes (for distant atolls) - scenic aerial views included
  • Domestic Flight + Speedboat: For far atolls (arranged by resort)
  • All transfers are pre-arranged and included in the package

Important Notes

  • Seaplane transfers operate only during daylight hours (6 AM - 4 PM)
  • Late arrivals may require overnight in Malé (can be arranged)
  • Valid passport required (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Maldives visa on arrival is free for 30 days
  • Check-in time: 3 hours before international departure
