Discover Kuala Lumpur, Ipoh & Penang

From USD 1,885 Per Person Sharing
8 Days / 7 Nights Duration
International Package Type
KL, Ipoh & Penang 3 Cities

Package Overview

Embark on an unforgettable 8-day journey through three of Malaysia's most captivating destinations. From the modern metropolis of Kuala Lumpur to the heritage charm of Ipoh and the cultural richness of Penang, this comprehensive multi-city tour showcases the best of Malaysia's diversity.

Package Highlights

  • 3 Iconic Cities: Experience Kuala Lumpur, Ipoh, and Penang in one journey
  • Sunway Lagoon Theme Park: Full-day 6-in-1 park admission with water rides and attractions
  • Lost World of Tambun Night Park: Unique evening adventure in Ipoh's natural hot springs resort
  • Penang Hill Funicular Train: Historic railway journey to panoramic island views
  • Comprehensive City Tours: 4-hour KL tour, 4-hour Ipoh tour, 8-hour Penang tour
  • Private Transfers Throughout: Comfortable air-conditioned vehicle with English driver/guide
  • Strategic Inter-City Travel: KL → Ipoh → Penang → KL circuit
  • 7 Nights Quality Accommodation: IBIS KLCC (4 nights), Sunway Lost World Tambun (1 night), Flamingo Beach Penang (2 nights)

City Highlights

🏙️ Kuala Lumpur

  • Modern skyscrapers and colonial architecture
  • Petronas Twin Towers and KLCC Park
  • Sunway Lagoon mega theme park
  • Shopping and entertainment districts

🏛️ Ipoh

  • Heritage buildings and colonial charm
  • Famous limestone caves and temples
  • Lost World of Tambun Night Park
  • Renowned local cuisine and white coffee

🏝️ Penang

  • UNESCO World Heritage George Town
  • Penang Hill panoramic views
  • Street art and cultural diversity
  • World-famous street food scene

Important Information

  • Valid passport required (minimum 6 months validity)

  • Total driving time KL to Ipoh: approximately 2.5 hours

  • Penang to KL return: approximately 5-6 hours
  • Sunway Lagoon: Bring swimwear, towel, and sunscreen
  • Lost World Tambun: Evening visit with natural hot springs
  • Penang Hill: Funicular train operates weather permitting
  • Comfortable walking shoes recommended for city tours

Detailed 8-Day Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival in Kuala Lumpur
+

Highlights: Airport Arrival | Private Transfer | KLCC Area

  • Arrive at Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA)
  • Meet and greet by English-speaking driver/guide
  • Private transfer to IBIS KLCC Hotel
  • Check-in at hotel (standard check-in: 3:00 PM)
  • Room allocation and welcome briefing
  • Receive multi-city tour information pack
  • Rest of day at leisure to:
    • Walk to Petronas Twin Towers (5 minutes walk)
    • Explore KLCC Park and fountain show
    • Visit Suria KLCC shopping mall
    • Dine at nearby restaurants
  • Overnight at IBIS KLCC, Kuala Lumpur

Meals: None (arrival day)

Day 2
Kuala Lumpur City Tour
+

Highlights: 4-Hour Private City Tour | Major Landmarks | Cultural Sites

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel
  • 9:00 AM: Meet guide for 4-hour Kuala Lumpur City Tour
  • Tour Highlights:
    • Independence Square (Dataran Merdeka):
      • Historic site of Malaysian independence declaration
      • View the world's tallest free-standing flagpole (100m)
      • Sultan Abdul Samad Building (colonial Moorish architecture)
    • National Mosque (Masjid Negara):
      • Modern Islamic architecture with blue roof
      • 73-meter high minaret
      • Capacity for 15,000 worshippers
    • National Monument:
      • Bronze sculpture commemorating fallen heroes
      • Set in beautiful Lake Gardens
    • King's Palace (Istana Negara):
      • Official residence of Yang di-Pertuan Agong
      • Photo opportunity at grand entrance
    • Petronas Twin Towers Photo Stop:
      • World's tallest twin towers (451.9 meters)
      • 88 floors of architectural marvel
      • Best photo spots from KLCC Park
    • Thean Hou Temple:
      • Six-tiered Chinese temple on Robson Heights
      • Dedicated to Goddess Thean Hou
      • Panoramic city views
  • 1:00 PM: Return to hotel
  • Afternoon free for:
    • Shopping at Bukit Bintang
    • Visit Berjaya Times Square
    • Explore Chinatown and Little India
    • Relaxation at hotel
  • Overnight at IBIS KLCC, Kuala Lumpur

Meals: Breakfast

Day 3
Sunway Lagoon Theme Park Full Day
+

Highlights: Sunway Lagoon 6-in-1 Park | Water Rides | Theme Adventures

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel
  • 9:00 AM: Depart for Sunway Lagoon (30 minutes drive)
  • Sunway Lagoon Theme Park - 6 Parks in 1:
    • 1. Water Park:
      • Malaysia's longest Vuvuzela water slide
      • FlowRider surfing simulator
      • Wave pool with artificial waves
      • Multiple water slides and attractions
    • 2. Amusement Park:
      • Pirate's Revenge pendulum ride
      • Grand Canyon River Rapids
      • Colorado Splash log flume
      • Various thrill rides
    • 3. Wildlife Park:
      • Over 150 species of animals
      • Interactive animal encounters
      • Birds, reptiles, and mammals
    • 4. Extreme Park:
      • Bungee jumping
      • Go-kart racing
      • Paintball arena
      • ATV rides
    • 5. Scream Park:
      • Horror-themed attractions
      • Haunted houses
      • Spooky experiences
    • 6. Nickelodeon Lost Lagoon:
      • Kid-friendly water attractions
      • Meet Nickelodeon characters
      • Interactive water play areas
  • Full day to explore all parks at your own pace
  • Lunch and refreshments available at park restaurants (own expense)
  • Lockers and changing facilities available
  • 6:00 PM: Depart Sunway Lagoon
  • 7:00 PM: Return to hotel
  • Overnight at IBIS KLCC, Kuala Lumpur

Meals: Breakfast

What to Bring: Swimwear, towel, extra clothes, sunscreen, waterproof bag for valuables, comfortable footwear

Day 4
Transfer to Ipoh + Lost World of Tambun Night Park
+

Highlights: Scenic Drive to Ipoh | Lost World of Tambun Night Park | Natural Hot Springs

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel
  • Check-out from IBIS KLCC
  • 10:00 AM: Begin private transfer from Kuala Lumpur to Ipoh
  • Journey Details:
    • Approximately 2.5 hours scenic drive (210 km)
    • Travel along North-South Expressway
    • Pass through Perak state countryside
    • Rest stop at highway service area
  • 12:30 PM: Arrive in Ipoh, heritage capital of Perak
  • Check-in at Sunway Lost World of Tambun Hotel
  • Lunch at hotel or nearby (own expense)
  • Afternoon: Free time to enjoy hotel facilities:
    • Swimming pool
    • Natural hot springs pools
    • Relaxation areas
    • Explore hotel surroundings
  • Evening - Lost World of Tambun Night Park:
    • Access to themed night park adjacent to hotel
    • Night Park Features:
      • Luminous Forest with glowing installations
      • Natural hot springs pools (bring swimwear)
      • Hot springs spa (7 therapeutic pools)
      • Water park attractions illuminated at night
      • Petting zoo with nocturnal animals
      • Cooling limestone cave exploration
      • Unique evening atmosphere under stars
    • Duration: 3-4 hours at leisure
  • Dinner at hotel or park restaurants (own expense)
  • Overnight at Sunway Lost World of Tambun Hotel, Ipoh

Meals: Breakfast

Special Experience: The hot springs water is naturally heated by underground geothermal activity - perfect for evening relaxation!

Day 5
Ipoh City Tour + Transfer to Penang
+

Highlights: 4-Hour Ipoh City Tour | Heritage Buildings | Transfer to Penang Island

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel
  • Check-out from Sunway Lost World of Tambun Hotel
  • 9:00 AM: Begin 4-hour Ipoh City Tour
  • Ipoh City Tour Highlights:
    • Ipoh Railway Station:
      • Nicknamed "Taj Mahal of Ipoh"
      • Beautiful colonial architecture
      • Built in 1917, stunning white building
    • Ipoh Old Town Heritage Walk:
      • Colonial-era shophouses
      • Street art murals
      • Traditional coffee shops
      • Historic Birch Memorial Clock Tower
    • Sam Poh Tong Cave Temple:
      • Buddhist temple inside natural limestone cave
      • Stalactites and stalagmites formations
      • Koi pond and beautiful gardens
      • Peaceful meditation sanctuary
    • Perak Cave Temple (Kek Lok Tong):
      • Another stunning cave temple
      • Zen garden with lake views
      • Buddhist shrines and statues
    • Concubine Lane (Lorong Panglima):
      • Historic narrow alley
      • Souvenir shops and cafes
      • Street food vendors
      • Perfect for photos
    • Photo opportunities:
      • Ipoh Town Hall
      • High Court building
      • St. Michael's Institution
  • 1:00 PM: Complete Ipoh tour
  • Lunch stop (optional - own expense) to try famous Ipoh white coffee and local delicacies
  • 2:00 PM: Depart Ipoh for Penang
  • Journey to Penang:
    • Approximately 2-hour drive (130 km)
    • Cross the iconic 13.5 km Penang Bridge
    • Second longest bridge in Southeast Asia
    • Spectacular views of the Straits of Malacca
  • 4:00 PM: Arrive in Penang
  • Check-in at Flamingo by the Beach Hotel
  • Rest of evening at leisure:
    • Enjoy beachfront location
    • Swim in hotel pool
    • Walk along the beach
    • Explore nearby restaurants
  • Overnight at Flamingo by the Beach, Penang

Meals: Breakfast

Ipoh Specialties to Try: Ipoh white coffee, chicken rice, tau fu fa (soy pudding), salted chicken, curry mee

Day 6
Penang City Tour with Penang Hill
+

Highlights: 8-Hour Penang Tour | George Town UNESCO Site | Penang Hill Funicular Train

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel with sea views
  • 9:00 AM: Meet guide for comprehensive 8-hour Penang City Tour
  • George Town UNESCO Heritage Site:
    • Inscribed as UNESCO World Heritage Site in 2008
    • Unique blend of British, Chinese, Malay, and Indian influences
    • Street Art Trail:
      • "Little Children on a Bicycle" by Ernest Zacharevic
      • "Brother and Sister on a Swing"
      • Interactive wire art installations
      • Marking George Town Heritage areas
    • Clan Jetties:
      • Traditional Chinese water villages on stilts
      • Chew Jetty (most famous)
      • Over 100 years of history
      • Unique kampung lifestyle
    • Heritage Buildings:
      • Fort Cornwallis (oldest British fort)
      • Penang City Hall
      • Colonial mansions along Jalan Penang
  • Religious & Cultural Sites:
    • Kek Lok Si Temple:
      • Largest Buddhist temple in Southeast Asia
      • Seven-tier Pagoda of Rama VI
      • Giant bronze Kuan Yin statue (36.5 meters)
      • Beautiful hillside location
    • Khoo Kongsi:
      • Ornate Chinese clan house
      • Intricate architecture and carvings
      • One of finest in Malaysia
    • Little India:
      • Vibrant Indian quarter
      • Spice shops and textile stores
      • Authentic Indian cuisine
  • Penang Hill Funicular Train Experience:
    • Board historic funicular railway (opened 1923)
    • Asia's earliest funicular train service
    • Ascend 833 meters above sea level
    • 30-minute ride through tropical rainforest
    • At the Summit:
      • Panoramic 360° views of Penang Island
      • View George Town cityscape
      • See Penang Bridge from above
      • Cooler temperature (5-7°C lower than below)
      • The Habitat nature area (optional)
      • Colonial bungalows and gardens
      • Restaurants and viewing decks
  • Additional Stops:
    • Penang Peranakan Mansion (optional entrance)
    • Chew Jetty waterfront
    • Armenian Street heritage area
  • 5:00 PM: Return to hotel
  • Evening at leisure - explore Penang's famous street food scene
  • Overnight at Flamingo by the Beach, Penang

Meals: Breakfast

Penang Street Food Must-Try: Char kway teow, assam laksa, nasi kandar, cendol, rojak, apom manis

Day 7
Transfer Back to Kuala Lumpur
+

Highlights: Scenic Return Journey | Free Evening in KL

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel
  • Final moments enjoying beach and hotel facilities
  • Check-out from Flamingo by the Beach Hotel
  • 10:00 AM: Begin return journey to Kuala Lumpur
  • Journey Details:
    • Approximately 5-6 hours drive (350 km)
    • Travel back across Penang Bridge
    • Scenic drive through Malaysian countryside
    • Comfort stops along North-South Expressway
    • Lunch stop at highway rest area (own expense)
  • 4:00 PM: Arrive back in Kuala Lumpur
  • Check-in at IBIS KLCC Hotel (return to same hotel)
  • Evening at leisure:
    • Last-minute shopping at Bukit Bintang
    • Visit Pavilion KL or Suria KLCC
    • Enjoy night view of Petronas Towers
    • Farewell dinner at favorite restaurant (own expense)
    • Jalan Alor food street experience
    • Pack and prepare for departure
  • Overnight at IBIS KLCC, Kuala Lumpur

Meals: Breakfast

Tip: Use this evening to purchase last-minute souvenirs - batik, pewter items, local snacks, white coffee

Day 8
Departure from Kuala Lumpur
+

Highlights: Final Moments | Airport Transfer | Homeward Journey

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel
  • Check-out from IBIS KLCC (standard check-out: 12:00 PM)
  • Time permitting before departure:
    • Last walk around KLCC Park
    • Final photos with Petronas Towers
    • Quick souvenir shopping at Suria KLCC
    • Coffee at local cafe
  • Private transfer to Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA)
  • Recommended arrival: 3 hours before international flight
  • Check-in for Qatar Airways return flight
  • Duty-free shopping at KLIA (world-class airport facilities)
  • Departure on Qatar Airways flight home
  • End of unforgettable 3-city Malaysian adventure!

Meals: Breakfast

Terima Kasih & Selamat Jalan! Thank you for choosing Jetwide Consortium. Your journey through Kuala Lumpur, Ipoh, and Penang has showcased the incredible diversity of Malaysia. We hope you return soon!
