5-Day Kuala Lumpur Shopping Paradise

FROM USD 1,265
DURATION 5 Days / 4 Nights
TOUR TYPE International
DESTINATION Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Package Overview

Discover the ultimate shopping paradise in Kuala Lumpur! This specially curated 5-day package is designed for shopping enthusiasts, featuring exclusive visits to Malaysia's premier shopping destinations including Genting Premium Outlet, Mitsui Outlet Park KLIA, and a comprehensive KL shopping tour with special discount vouchers.

Package Highlights

  • Genting Premium Outlet (GPO): Shop international brands at discounted prices
  • 5-Hour KL Shopping Tour: Explore the best shopping districts with exclusive discount vouchers
  • Mitsui Outlet Park KLIA: Airport shopping experience enroute to departure
  • Genting Highlands Experience: Scenic cable car ride with panoramic views
  • Batu Caves Visit: Iconic cultural landmark and Hindu shrine
  • Shopping Discount Vouchers: Save more on your purchases at select stores
  • Qatar Airways: Quality airline service with comfortable travel
  • Strategic Hotel Location: The Pearl Kuala Lumpur Hotel - perfect for shoppers

Important Information

  • Valid passport required (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Comfortable walking shoes recommended for shopping tours
  • Shopping vouchers provided upon arrival
  • Additional baggage allowance can be arranged for purchases
  • Most shops accept major credit cards and international currencies
  • GST refund available for tourists at participating outlets
  • Free time allows for personal exploration and additional shopping
  • Hotel location provides easy access to nearby shopping areas

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival in Kuala Lumpur
+

Highlights: Airport Arrival | Hotel Check-in | Leisure Time

  • Arrive at Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA)
  • Meet and greet by our representative
  • Transfer to The Pearl Kuala Lumpur Hotel
  • Check-in and room allocation
  • Receive your shopping discount vouchers at reception
  • Rest of the day at leisure to relax or explore nearby areas
  • Optional: Evening stroll to Bukit Bintang shopping area
  • Shopping Tip: Most malls open until 10:00 PM
  • Overnight stay at The Pearl Kuala Lumpur Hotel

Meals: None (arrival day)

Day 2
Genting Highlands Tour with Batu Caves
+

Highlights: Batu Caves | Genting Highlands | Cable Car Ride | Genting Premium Outlet

  • 8:30 AM: Breakfast at hotel
  • 9:00 AM: Departure for full-day Genting Highlands Tour
  • First Stop - Batu Caves:
    • Visit the famous Batu Caves Hindu shrine
    • Climb the 272 colorful steps to the main cave temple
    • Admire the 140-foot golden Lord Murugan statue
    • Explore the limestone cave formations
    • Photo opportunities with monkeys and pigeons
    • Duration: approximately 1 hour
  • Journey to Genting Highlands:
    • Scenic drive through lush rainforest
    • Ascend to 6,000 feet above sea level
    • Experience cooler mountain climate
  • Genting Highlands Experience:
    • Two-Way Cable Car Ride: Enjoy panoramic views of tropical rainforest
    • Arrive at Awana SkyCentral station
    • Experience the longest and fastest cable car in Southeast Asia
    • Photo stops at scenic viewpoints
  • Genting Premium Outlet (GPO) Shopping:
    • Transfer to Genting Premium Outlet
    • Shop at over 150 international and local brands
    • Enjoy discounts from 25% to 65% off retail prices
    • Brands include: Nike, Adidas, Coach, Michael Kors, Polo Ralph Lauren, and more
    • Complimentary shopping tote bag at selected stores
    • Duration: approximately 3 hours
  • 6:00 PM: Return journey to Kuala Lumpur
  • 7:30 PM: Arrive at hotel
  • Evening at leisure - rest or continue shopping at nearby malls
  • Overnight stay at The Pearl Kuala Lumpur Hotel

Meals: Breakfast

Shopping Note: Bring comfortable shoes and extra bags for purchases. Currency exchange available at outlet.

Day 3
Kuala Lumpur Shopping Tour (5 Hours)
+

Highlights: City Shopping Districts | Discount Vouchers | Major Malls | Local Markets

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel
  • 10:00 AM: Departure for 5-hour Kuala Lumpur Shopping Tour
  • Tour Includes:
    • Pavilion KL:
      • Premium shopping mall with luxury brands
      • Over 450 retail outlets
      • International fashion houses and local designers
      • Use your discount vouchers at participating stores
    • Suria KLCC:
      • Located at base of Petronas Twin Towers
      • High-end brands and electronics
      • Kinokuniya bookstore - largest in Malaysia
      • Photo opportunity at KLCC Park
    • Bukit Bintang Shopping Belt:
      • Visit Lot 10, Fahrenheit 88, and Starhill Gallery
      • Street shopping at Jalan Alor area
      • Local and international brands
      • Souvenir shops and handicraft stores
    • Central Market:
      • Traditional Malaysian handicrafts
      • Batik fabrics and local artwork
      • Souvenirs and gifts
      • Cultural performances (weekend)
  • Shopping Discount Vouchers Included:
    • Fashion and apparel stores
    • Electronics and gadgets
    • Beauty and cosmetics
    • Sports and lifestyle brands
    • Terms and conditions apply - voucher booklet provided
  • 3:00 PM: Return to hotel
  • Afternoon/Evening: Free time for:
    • Rest and organize your purchases
    • Additional personal shopping
    • Visit nearby Petronas Towers fountain show (evening)
    • Explore local street food at Jalan Alor
  • Overnight stay at The Pearl Kuala Lumpur Hotel

Meals: Breakfast

Pro Shopping Tips:

  • 💡 Most stores offer GST tourist refund - keep receipts
  • 💡 Bargaining accepted at Central Market and street vendors
  • 💡 Fixed prices at major malls
  • 💡 Credit cards widely accepted

  • Evening Suggestions:

  • Overnight stay at The Pearl Kuala Lumpur Hotel

Meals: Breakfast

Note: Optional activities can be arranged through hotel concierge or tour desk. Our representative can assist with bookings.

Day 5
Departure via Mitsui Outlet Park KLIA
+

Highlights: Final Shopping | Mitsui Outlet Park | Airport Transfer | Departure

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel
  • Check-out from hotel (standard time: 12:00 PM)
  • Last Minute Shopping: Time permitting before departure
  • Transfer to Airport via Mitsui Outlet Park KLIA:
    • Strategic stop at Malaysia's first international premium outlet
    • Located just 15 minutes from KLIA
    • Shopping Duration: 2-3 hours (depending on flight time)
    • Outlet Features:
      • Over 130 designer and brand-name shops
      • Discounts from 25% to 65% off
      • Brands: Armani, Brooks Brothers, Calvin Klein, Kate Spade, and more
      • Luggage and travel bags available for purchases
      • GST refund counter available
    • Convenient location allows stress-free last-minute shopping
    • Luggage storage facilities available
  • Continue to Airport:
    • 15-minute drive to KLIA
    • Check-in for Qatar Airways flight
    • Recommended arrival: 3 hours before departure
  • Departure: Board your Qatar Airways flight home
  • End of unforgettable Kuala Lumpur shopping experience!

Meals: Breakfast

Packing Tip: Remember to pack purchases in checked luggage. Additional baggage allowance can be purchased if needed. Keep receipts for GST refund at airport.
