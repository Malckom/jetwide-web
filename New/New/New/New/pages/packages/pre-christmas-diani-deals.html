PRE-CHRISTMAS COASTAL DEALS - Diani Beach

FROM KSh 20,800
DURATION 3 Nights
VALID UNTIL 20th Dec 2025
DESTINATION Diani Beach, Kenya

Package Overview

Experience the magic of Diani Beach before the festive rush! Known for its stunning white sandy beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters, Diani is consistently rated as one of Africa's best beaches. This 3-night package offers incredible value with 6 exceptional resort options, all featuring the famous Diani Beach experience.

📍 Location: Diani Beach, South Coast
⏰ Booking Period: Valid until 20th December 2025
🚆 Transport: Return SGR + Ukunda transfers

Package Highlights

  • 3 nights accommodation at your choice of Diani resort
  • Meals as per hotel meal plan (BB, Half Board, or All-Inclusive)
  • Return SGR train tickets from Nairobi to Mombasa
  • Road transfers to Diani Beach via Likoni Ferry (~1.5 hours)
  • Access to 25km of pristine white sandy beach
  • Pre-Christmas special rates (save up to 40%)
  • 6 resort options from budget to luxury

Why Choose Diani Beach?

  • Best Beach in Africa: Consistently ranked among the world's top beaches
  • Perfect for All: Family-friendly resorts with activities for everyone
  • Amazing Value: Starting from just KSh 20,800 for 3 nights
  • All-Inclusive Options: Multiple AI resorts to choose from
  • Water Sports Hub: Kite surfing, diving, snorkeling, and more

Activities & Attractions

  • Swimming in the warm Indian Ocean
  • Kite surfing (Diani is a world-famous kite surfing destination)
  • Snorkeling and scuba diving at Kisite-Mpunguti Marine Park
  • Shimba Hills National Reserve day trips
  • Colobus monkey conservation center
  • Diani Beach art gallery and crafts market
  • Water sports: jet skiing, parasailing, kayaking
  • Sunset dhow cruises
  • Local Swahili cuisine and fresh seafood

Important Notes

  • Prices are per person sharing (double occupancy)
  • Single room supplement available on request
  • Child rates available - contact us for details
  • Valid for travel until 20th December 2025
  • Transfer includes Likoni Ferry crossing
  • Advance booking recommended for preferred resort choice
  • Additional nights can be added at special rates
