Diani Budget Beach Getaway

Escape to Kenya's most beautiful beach without breaking the bank! Our Diani Budget Getaway offers the perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and value for money.

Enjoy 4 days of sun, sand, and sea at one of Africa's top-rated beaches. With crystal-clear turquoise waters, powder-white sand, and swaying palm trees, Diani Beach is the perfect tropical paradise for couples, families, and solo travelers.

Location:

Diani Beach, South Coast - Ukunda, Kwale County

  • 30km south of Mombasa
  • 17km of pristine white sand beach
  • Warm Indian Ocean waters
  • World-class water sports facilities
  • Vibrant nightlife and restaurants

Highlights

  • Beach volleyball and football
  • Snorkeling in coral reefs
  • Visit to Shimba Hills National Reserve (optional)
  • Kite surfing and jet ski rides
  • Glass-bottom boat tours
  • Local seafood barbecues
  • Sunset dhow cruises
  • Shopping at Diani Beach Art Gallery

What's Included

  • 3 nights accommodation in budget-friendly hotel
  • Daily breakfast
  • Return SGR train tickets OR road transport from Nairobi
  • Airport/station transfers
  • Beach access
  • Welcome refreshment

Optional Add-Ons

  • Shimba Hills day trip - KSh 8,000
  • Dolphin watching - KSh 5,000
  • Kite surfing lessons - KSh 6,500
  • Scuba diving - KSh 12,000

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Diani

Begin your beach adventure!

  • Pick-up from Nairobi via SGR or road transport
  • Scenic journey through coastal landscapes
  • Arrive at your hotel and check-in
  • Welcome refreshment
  • Afternoon at leisure on the beach
  • Sunset walk along the shore
  • Dinner at hotel (own expense)

Meals: Breakfast (if road transport) | Overnight: Diani Beach

Day 2: Full Day Beach Relaxation

Enjoy the best of Diani Beach!

  • Leisurely breakfast at hotel
  • Morning swim in the warm Indian Ocean
  • Beach games - volleyball, frisbee, football
  • Try local snacks from beach vendors
  • Optional: Jet ski or kite surfing (extra cost)
  • Afternoon siesta or book reading under palm trees
  • Evening beach barbecue (optional)
  • Live music at nearby beach bars

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: Diani Beach

Day 3: Adventure Day (Optional Activities)

Choose your own adventure!

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Option 1: Shimba Hills National Reserve day trip (see elephants, sable antelopes)
  • Option 2: Dolphin watching excursion
  • Option 3: Snorkeling at Kisite-Mpunguti Marine Park
  • Option 4: Relaxation & spa treatments at hotel
  • Afternoon shopping at Diani Beach Shopping Center
  • Sunset dhow cruise (optional)
  • Dinner at Ali Barbour's Cave Restaurant (optional, extra cost)

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: Diani Beach

Day 4: Departure

Last moments in paradise.

  • Final breakfast with ocean views
  • Morning beach walk & photo session
  • Check-out from hotel
  • Last-minute souvenir shopping
  • Transfer to Ukunda airstrip or Mombasa SGR station
  • Depart for Nairobi with sun-kissed memories

Meals: Breakfast

Transport Options

Choose the transport option that suits your budget and schedule.

1. SGR Train (Recommended)

  • Route: Nairobi to Mombasa (Madaraka Express)
  • Duration: 4.5 - 5 hours
  • Class: Economy or First Class
  • Departure: Morning departures available
  • Transfer: Taxi from Mombasa to Diani (1 hour)

2. Road Transport

  • Modern buses: Modern Coast, Mombasa Raha, Tahmeed
  • Duration: 8-9 hours
  • Departure: Evening/night buses available
  • Drop-off: Ukunda or Diani Beach

3. Flight (Upgrade Option)

  • Airlines: Jambojet, Skyward Express
  • Duration: 1 hour
  • Route: Nairobi (JKIA/Wilson) to Ukunda Airstrip
  • Additional Cost: USD 120-150 per person (return)

Note: Package price includes SGR Economy OR road transport. Flight upgrade available at extra cost.

Baggage: 20kg luggage allowance for SGR/Bus | 15kg for flight
