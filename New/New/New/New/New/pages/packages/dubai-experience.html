Package Overview

Experience the magic of Dubai with this comprehensive 5-day package featuring the city's iconic attractions, desert adventure, and cultural experiences. This carefully curated itinerary includes return flights on Fly Emirates, Visa Services, comfortable accommodation, and guided tours of Dubai's most spectacular sites.

Package Highlights

  • Burj Khalifa Views: Marvel at the world's tallest building and explore Dubai Mall
  • Desert Safari Adventure: Thrilling dune bashing, camel rides, and traditional BBQ dinner under the stars
  • Marina Dhow Cruise: Romantic evening cruise with international buffet dinner
  • Dubai City Tour: Half-day exploration of Dubai's landmarks and cultural sites
  • Premium Locations: Stay in well-located hotels with easy access to attractions
  • Hassle-Free Travel: Includes Visa Services, airport transfers, and tourism dirham
  • Flexible Options: Choose from 6 hotel categories to suit your budget

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival in Dubai
+

Highlights: Airport Transfer | Hotel Check-in | Leisure Time

  • Arrival at Dubai International Airport
  • Meet and greet by our representative
  • Private transfer to your selected hotel
  • Check-in and room allocation (standard check-in: 3:00 PM)
  • Rest of the day at leisure to explore nearby areas or relax at the hotel
  • Optional: Visit Dubai Mall or take a stroll around your hotel neighborhood
  • Overnight stay in Dubai

Meals: None (arrival day)

Day 2
Half-Day Dubai City Tour
+

Highlights: Burj Al Arab | Jumeirah Beach | Dubai Museum | Gold & Spice Souks

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel
  • Pick-up for half-day Dubai city tour (shared basis)
  • Photo stop at Burj Al Arab: Iconic sail-shaped luxury hotel
  • Jumeirah Beach: Scenic coastline with views of Burj Al Arab
  • Jumeirah Mosque: Photo opportunity at Dubai's most beautiful mosque
  • Al Fahidi Historical District: Explore Dubai's heritage quarter
  • Dubai Museum: Visit Al Fahidi Fort and learn about Dubai's history
  • Gold Souk: Browse through glittering displays of gold jewelry
  • Spice Souk: Experience aromatic traditional Arabian spices
  • Return to hotel by early afternoon
  • Rest of day free for shopping or personal exploration
  • Overnight stay in Dubai

Meals: Breakfast

Day 3
Desert Safari with BBQ Dinner
+

Highlights: Dune Bashing | Camel Riding | Sunset Views | BBQ Dinner | Live Entertainment

  • Morning & Afternoon: Breakfast at hotel and day at leisure
  • Optional activities: Visit Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall shopping, or beach relaxation
  • 3:00 PM: Pick-up from hotel for desert safari adventure (shared basis)
  • Drive to the golden sand dunes of Arabian Desert
  • Dune Bashing: Thrilling 4x4 desert drive over sand dunes (approx. 30 minutes)
  • Photo Opportunities: Capture stunning desert sunset views
  • Arrive at traditional Bedouin-style desert camp
  • Camp Activities:
    • Camel riding experience
    • Henna painting (for ladies)
    • Traditional Arabic costume photo session
    • Shisha smoking (hookah)
    • Sand boarding
  • Dinner: Delicious BBQ buffet with vegetarian and non-vegetarian options
  • Live Entertainment:
    • Tanoura dance show (whirling dervish)
    • Fire show
    • Belly dancing performance
  • Return to hotel by 10:00 PM
  • Overnight stay in Dubai

Meals: Breakfast, BBQ Dinner

Day 4
Marina Dhow Cruise Dinner
+

Highlights: Dubai Marina | Traditional Dhow Boat | International Buffet | Entertainment

  • Morning & Afternoon: Breakfast at hotel
  • Full day at leisure for personal exploration
  • Suggested Activities:
    • Visit The Palm Jumeirah and Atlantis Hotel
    • Explore Mall of the Emirates or Dubai Mall
    • Relax at Jumeirah Beach
    • Visit Global Village (seasonal)
    • Shopping at traditional souks
  • 7:00 PM: Pick-up from hotel for Marina Dhow Cruise (shared basis)
  • Welcome drink upon boarding
  • Cruise Experience:
    • Sail along Dubai Marina waterfront
    • Admire illuminated skyline and modern architecture
    • View iconic landmarks from water perspective
    • 2-hour scenic cruise
  • Dinner: International buffet with Arabic and continental cuisine
  • Entertainment: Traditional music and Tanoura dance show
  • Return to hotel by 11:00 PM
  • Overnight stay in Dubai

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

Day 5
Departure
+

Highlights: Final Shopping | Airport Transfer | Departure

  • Morning: Breakfast at hotel
  • Check-out from hotel (standard check-out: 12:00 PM)
  • Time permitting: Last-minute shopping or sightseeing
  • Transfer to Dubai International Airport
  • Recommended arrival at airport 3 hours before international flight
  • Departure on Fly Emirates flight
  • End of memorable Dubai experience

Meals: Breakfast
