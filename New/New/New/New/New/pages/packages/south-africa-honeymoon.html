Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Cape Town

Meals: None | Overnight: Cape Town

Arrive at Cape Town International Airport and transfer to your hotel. Rest and freshen up. In the afternoon, optional visit to the vibrant V&A Waterfront for shopping, dining, and harbor views. Watch the sunset from the waterfront while enjoying a romantic dinner at one of the many excellent restaurants.

Day 2: Cape Town City Tour & Table Mountain

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: Cape Town

After breakfast, embark on a city tour. Visit the Company's Garden, drive through the historic Bo-Kaap neighborhood with colorful houses, and stop at Signal Hill for panoramic views. Take the rotating cable car to the summit of Table Mountain (weather permitting) for breathtaking 360-degree views of Cape Town, Table Bay, and surrounding peaks. Afternoon at leisure to explore Long Street, visit museums, or relax at Camps Bay beach.

Day 3: Cape Peninsula Full-Day Tour

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: Cape Town

Full-day tour of the spectacular Cape Peninsula. Drive along the scenic Chapman's Peak Drive with stunning coastal views. Visit Hout Bay and optional seal island boat cruise. Continue to Cape Point and the Cape of Good Hope Nature Reserve - walk to the lighthouse for incredible ocean views. Stop at Boulders Beach to see the famous African penguin colony. Return via Simon's Town and Fish Hoek, with optional stop in Kalk Bay fishing village.

Day 4: Stellenbosch Wine Tour

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: Stellenbosch

Drive to the beautiful Cape Winelands. Visit 3-4 award-winning wine estates in Stellenbosch for wine tasting and cellar tours. Enjoy the stunning mountain scenery and Cape Dutch architecture. Have lunch at a wine estate (own expense). Walk through the charming historic town of Stellenbosch with its oak-lined streets. Check into your Stellenbosch hotel and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 5: Fly to Kruger - Afternoon Game Drive

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner | Overnight: Kruger Safari Lodge

Morning transfer to Cape Town Airport for your flight to Kruger Mpumalanga International Airport (or Hoedspruit). Meet your lodge transfer and drive to your safari lodge (approximately 1-2 hours through beautiful Lowveld scenery). Arrive at the lodge for lunch and check-in. Freshen up and enjoy high tea before your first afternoon game drive (4:00 PM - sunset). Search for the Big Five and other wildlife with experienced rangers. Enjoy sundowner drinks in the bush before returning to the lodge for dinner and overnight.

Day 6: Full-Day Kruger Safari

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Kruger Safari Lodge

Early morning wake-up call (5:30 AM) with coffee and rusks before your sunrise game drive. The early morning is prime time to spot predators and enjoy the African bush coming to life. Return to the lodge around 9:00 AM for brunch. Mid-morning and afternoon at leisure - relax by the pool, enjoy spa treatments (optional), or take a siesta. Afternoon game drive departs around 4:00 PM. Track wildlife as they become active in the cooler evening hours. Enjoy another magical bush sundowner. Return to the lodge for dinner under the stars.

Day 7: Morning Game Drive & Fly to Johannesburg

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: Johannesburg

Final early morning game drive to make the most of your safari experience. Return to the lodge for breakfast and check-out. Transfer to the airport for your flight to Johannesburg. Arrive and transfer to your airport hotel. Rest of the day at leisure. Optional visit to nearby Sandton City for last-minute shopping. Overnight near OR Tambo International Airport.

Day 8: Departure

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: N/A

Breakfast at the hotel. Depending on your flight time, you may have some time for last-minute shopping or relaxation. Transfer to OR Tambo International Airport for your flight back to Nairobi, carrying wonderful memories of your South African honeymoon adventure.
