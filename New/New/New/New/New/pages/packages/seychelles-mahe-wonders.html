Holiday Overview

Escape to paradise with our 5-day Seychelles Mahe Wonders package. Discover the largest and most vibrant island in the Seychelles archipelago, where pristine white-sand beaches meet lush tropical forests and granite boulder-strewn coastlines.

Mahe Island offers the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. Whether you're looking to unwind on some of the world's most beautiful beaches, explore the colorful capital city of Victoria, or immerse yourself in the rich Creole culture, this short break provides an unforgettable introduction to the Seychelles' natural beauty and warm hospitality.

Experience Highlights

  • Relax on world-famous beaches including Beau Vallon, Anse Intendance, and Anse Takamaka
  • Explore Victoria, the world's smallest capital city, with its colorful markets and colonial architecture
  • Discover Morne Seychellois National Park with its scenic hiking trails and panoramic views
  • Snorkel in crystal-clear waters teeming with tropical fish and marine life
  • Visit the famous Sir Selwyn Selwyn-Clarke Market for local fruits, spices, and crafts
  • Experience authentic Creole cuisine blending African, French, Chinese, and Indian influences
  • Photograph iconic granite boulders and palm-fringed beaches
  • Enjoy water sports including kayaking, paddleboarding, and diving (optional activities)

Important Notes

  • Climate: Seychelles has a tropical climate. Best weather is April-May and October-November. Expect warm temperatures year-round (24-32¬∞C).
  • Visa: Visitors receive a free visitor's permit on arrival valid for up to 3 months. No pre-arranged visa required.
  • Currency: Seychellois Rupee (SCR). US Dollars and Euros widely accepted. Credit cards accepted at most hotels and restaurants.
  • Language: Creole, English, and French are official languages. English is widely spoken in tourist areas.
  • Time Zone: Seychelles is GMT+4 (1 hour ahead of Kenya).
  • Health: No mandatory vaccinations. Bring reef-safe sunscreen, insect repellent, and stay hydrated.
  • Activities: Book water sports and excursions through your hotel. Popular activities include snorkeling, diving, fishing, and island hopping.
  • Beach Safety: Always swim at beaches with lifeguards. Be aware of strong currents during monsoon seasons (May-Sep and Nov-Mar).

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Mahe - Welcome to Paradise

Welcome to Seychelles! Upon arrival at Seychelles International Airport on Mahe Island, you'll be greeted with the warm tropical air and the friendly smiles of the Seychellois people.

After clearing customs and collecting your luggage, you'll be met by our representative who will assist with your airport transfer to your hotel. The journey offers your first glimpses of Mahe's stunning scenery ‚Äì lush green mountains, azure waters, and pristine beaches.

Check in to your hotel and take time to settle in and refresh. Depending on your arrival time, you might want to:

  • Take a refreshing dip in the hotel pool
  • Stroll to the nearest beach for your first Seychelles sunset
  • Explore the immediate surroundings of your hotel
  • Enjoy a welcome dinner at a local Creole restaurant (optional, at own expense)

Rest well tonight ‚Äì your Seychelles adventure begins tomorrow!

Accommodation: Selected hotel in Mahe

Meals: None (breakfast from Day 2)

Transfers: Airport to hotel included

Day 2

Victoria City Tour & Beach Exploration

After breakfast, spend the morning exploring Victoria, the charming capital city of Seychelles and the smallest capital in the world.

Suggested Victoria highlights (self-guided or optional tour):

  • Sir Selwyn Selwyn-Clarke Market - Browse stalls selling fresh fish, tropical fruits, vegetables, and spices. The perfect place to experience local life and pick up souvenirs
  • Clock Tower - Photograph the iconic silver-painted replica of London's Big Ben clock tower
  • Seychelles National Museum - Learn about the islands' history, culture, and natural heritage
  • Botanical Gardens - Wander through beautiful gardens featuring endemic palms, spice plants, and giant tortoises
  • Hindu Temple - Visit the colorful Arul Mihu Navasakthi Vinayagar Temple

In the afternoon, head to one of Mahe's famous beaches. Beau Vallon Beach is the most popular, offering excellent swimming, water sports, and beachside restaurants. Alternatively, explore quieter beaches like Anse Royale or Anse Intendance.

Evening at leisure. Consider dining at one of the beachfront restaurants serving fresh seafood and Creole specialties.

Accommodation: Selected hotel in Mahe

Meals: Breakfast

Activities: Self-guided exploration (car rental or taxis recommended)

Day 3

Full Day at Leisure - Beach & Activities

Today is yours to enjoy as you wish! Breakfast at your hotel, then the day is completely free for your own activities.

Suggested activities (optional, not included):

  • Snorkeling Adventure - Join a boat trip to Sainte Anne Marine National Park or Baie Ternay. Equipment usually provided
  • Island Hopping - Take a day trip to nearby islands like Praslin or La Digue
  • Hiking - Trek through Morne Seychellois National Park. The Copolia Trail offers stunning panoramic views (4-5 hours round trip)
  • Beach Hopping - Rent a car and explore Mahe's 65+ beaches. Don't miss Anse Intendance, Anse Takamaka, and Petite Anse
  • Water Sports - Try kayaking, paddleboarding, jet skiing, or take scuba diving lessons
  • Spa Day - Indulge in a relaxing spa treatment at one of the island's luxury spas
  • Fishing Trip - Join a deep-sea fishing excursion

Alternatively, simply relax on the beach with a good book, swim in the warm Indian Ocean waters, and soak up the tropical sun.

In the evening, watch the spectacular Seychelles sunset ‚Äì the golden hour light makes for incredible photographs.

Accommodation: Selected hotel in Mahe

Meals: Breakfast

Activities: Day at leisure (activities at own expense)

Day 4

Coastal Drive & Southern Beaches

After breakfast, embark on a scenic coastal drive around Mahe (car rental recommended or book a guided island tour).

Suggested route highlights:

  • Takamaka Bay Distillery - Visit this rum distillery for a tour and tasting. Learn how Seychelles' famous rum is produced
  • Anse Intendance - One of Mahe's most beautiful beaches with powdery white sand and dramatic surf. Perfect for photography but be cautious swimming due to strong currents
  • Anse Takamaka - A stunning, palm-fringed beach ideal for swimming and sunbathing. Enjoy lunch at one of the beachside restaurants
  • Craft Village - Stop at local craft shops to see artisans creating traditional Seychellois crafts
  • Jardin du Roi - A spice garden with beautiful views, endemic plants, and a restaurant serving Creole cuisine

Continue your drive along the scenic coastal road, stopping at viewpoints and beaches that catch your eye. The southern coast of Mahe is less developed and offers a more authentic, tranquil experience.

Return to your hotel in the late afternoon. Enjoy your final evening in paradise with a special dinner (optional).

Accommodation: Selected hotel in Mahe

Meals: Breakfast

Activities: Self-guided coastal exploration

Day 5

Departure - Farewell to Paradise

Enjoy your final breakfast in Seychelles. Depending on your flight time, you may have a few hours to:

  • Take a last morning swim or beach walk
  • Do some final souvenir shopping
  • Relax by the pool
  • Enjoy one more Creole meal

Check out of your hotel and transfer to Seychelles International Airport for your departure flight. Our representative will ensure you arrive in plenty of time for check-in.

Depart with unforgettable memories of turquoise waters, pristine beaches, and the warm hospitality of the Seychellois people. Bon voyage and come back soon!

Meals: Breakfast

Transfers: Hotel to airport included

Note: If you'd like to extend your stay or add other islands (Praslin, La Digue), we can arrange custom extensions.
