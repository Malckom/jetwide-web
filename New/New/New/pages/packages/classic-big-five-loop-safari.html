Safari Overview

Experience the ultimate 8-day Kenya safari adventure, perfectly designed to showcase the country's most spectacular wildlife destinations and iconic attractions. This classic Big-Five circuit combines Nairobi's unique urban wildlife experiences with the natural wonders of Amboseli, the Great Rift Valley lakes, and the legendary Maasai Mara.

Why Choose This Safari?

This classic circuit represents the quintessential Kenya safari experience. From the unique juxtaposition of wildlife and cityscape in Nairobi National Park to the endless plains of the Maasai Mara, you'll experience the full spectrum of Kenya's natural and cultural heritage. The inclusion of Nairobi's conservation centers adds an educational dimension that sets this safari apart.

Best Time to Visit

Year-round destination with each season offering unique experiences. The dry seasons (January-February and June-October) provide optimal game viewing, while the Great Migration in the Mara (July-October) offers one of nature's most spectacular events.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival ‚Äì Nairobi Excursions

Meals: Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Bidwood Suites, Nairobi

On arrival at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) in the morning, you will be met by our representative and given a short safari briefing. After enjoying some light snacks, you will proceed to Nairobi National Park for a game drive, offering the unique opportunity to see lions, giraffes, rhinos, zebras, and many other species against the backdrop of Nairobi city.

From there, visit the David Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage, where rescued baby elephants are cared for before being reintroduced to the wild. Later, proceed to the Karen Blixen Coffee Garden for a delightful lunch.

In the afternoon, visit the Giraffe Centre, where you can interact with and feed the endangered Rothschild giraffes. By evening, you will be transferred to Bidwood Suites for dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Nairobi ‚Äì Amboseli National Park

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Sentrim Amboseli Lodge

After breakfast, depart Nairobi and drive south through the scenic Athi plains to Amboseli National Park. Arrive at Sentrim Amboseli Lodge in time for lunch and check-in.

In the late afternoon, enjoy your first game drive in the park, famous for its elephant herds and spectacular views of Mount Kilimanjaro. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

Day 3: Amboseli ‚Äì Full Day Game Drive

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Sentrim Amboseli Lodge

Today you will spend the entire day exploring Amboseli National Park. After an early breakfast, set out with your packed picnic lunch for a full-day game drive.

Explore the different habitats of Amboseli, from swamps to open plains, as you search for lions, cheetahs, buffalo, wildebeest, giraffes, and abundant birdlife. In the evening, return to Sentrim Amboseli Lodge for dinner and overnight.

Day 4: Amboseli ‚Äì Lake Naivasha

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Lake Naivasha Resort

After breakfast, depart Amboseli and drive via Nairobi into the Great Rift Valley, arriving at Lake Naivasha Resort in time for lunch.

In the afternoon, enjoy a boat ride on Lake Naivasha, where you will spot hippos, waterbirds, and beautiful scenery. Continue with a guided visit to Crescent Island Sanctuary, a picturesque spot where you can walk among giraffes, zebras, and antelopes in their natural habitat. Return to the resort in the evening for dinner and overnight.

Day 5: Lake Naivasha ‚Äì Lake Nakuru National Park

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Ziwa Bush Lodge

After breakfast, drive to Lake Nakuru National Park, famous for its rhino sanctuary and diverse birdlife. You will arrive in time for lunch at Ziwa Bush Lodge.

In the afternoon, embark on a game drive within the park, where you may encounter both black and white rhinos, giraffes, lions, leopards, and abundant wildlife along the shores of the lake. Return to the lodge in the evening for dinner and overnight.

Day 6: Lake Nakuru ‚Äì Maasai Mara National Reserve

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Zebra Plains Mara Camp

After breakfast, check out and depart for the legendary Maasai Mara National Reserve, driving through the scenic Rift Valley landscapes. You will arrive at Zebra Plains Mara Camp in time for lunch and check-in.

In the afternoon, head out for your first thrilling game drive in the Mara, where vast plains teem with wildlife. Later, return to the camp for dinner and overnight.

Day 7: Maasai Mara ‚Äì Full Day Game Drive

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Zebra Plains Mara Camp

After an early breakfast, set off for a full-day game drive with your packed picnic lunch. The day will be spent exploring the Mara's endless savannahs, riverine forests, and rolling hills in search of the Big Five and other predators.

During the migration season (July‚ÄìOctober), you may also witness the breathtaking wildebeest migration. Enjoy your picnic lunch in the wild before continuing your adventure. Return to Zebra Plains Mara Camp in the evening for dinner and overnight.

Day 8: Maasai Mara ‚Äì Nairobi ‚Äì Departure

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch | Overnight: N/A

After breakfast, check out and enjoy a leisurely drive back to Nairobi. En route, stop for a delicious lunch at The Ridge Cabin Resort.

Continue to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) in time for your international departure flight, marking the end of a memorable safari.
