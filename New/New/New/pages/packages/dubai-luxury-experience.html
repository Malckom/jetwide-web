Experience Dubai - Where Innovation Meets Tradition

Discover the dazzling city of Dubai, where futuristic skyscrapers stand alongside traditional souks, and golden deserts meet pristine beaches. This customizable Dubai experience offers the perfect blend of luxury, adventure, culture, and entertainment.

From the world's tallest building to man-made islands and record-breaking attractions, Dubai pushes the boundaries of what's possible. Shop in world-class malls, dine at Michelin-starred restaurants, explore ancient heritage sites, and experience thrilling desert adventures. Whether you're seeking luxury, family fun, shopping, or cultural experiences, Dubai delivers it all in spectacular style.

Dubai Experience Highlights

  • Visit Burj Khalifa - ascend to the 124th & 125th floor observation decks for panoramic views
  • Experience Dubai Mall - one of the world's largest shopping and entertainment destinations
  • Desert Safari adventure with dune bashing, camel riding, and BBQ dinner under the stars
  • Explore Dubai Marina and take a traditional dhow cruise
  • Visit the iconic Burj Al Arab and Palm Jumeirah
  • Discover Old Dubai - Gold Souk, Spice Souk, and Al Fahidi Historical District
  • Experience Dubai Fountain shows and The Beach at JBR
  • Optional: IMG Worlds of Adventure, Ski Dubai, Aquaventure Waterpark, Global Village

Important Notes

  • Climate: Dubai is extremely hot in summer (May-September, 40-50¬∞C). Best time to visit: November to March (pleasant 20-30¬∞C).
  • Visa: Tourist visa required. Can be arranged on arrival for many nationalities or through hotels. We assist with Visa Services.
  • Currency: UAE Dirham (AED). Credit cards widely accepted. ATMs available everywhere.
  • Dress Code: Modest dress appreciated in public places. Swimwear only at beaches/pools. Cover shoulders and knees in malls and traditional areas.
  • Alcohol: Available only in licensed restaurants, bars, and hotels. Not sold in regular stores. Drinking in public is prohibited.
  • Transportation: Excellent metro system, taxis widely available, ride-hailing apps (Uber, Careem) operate. Car rentals available.
  • Language: Arabic is official language, but English is widely spoken everywhere.
  • Ramadan: During the holy month, restaurants are closed during daylight hours. Eating/drinking in public during fasting hours is prohibited.
  • Safety: Dubai is extremely safe with very low crime rates. Women can travel alone safely.
  • Tipping: 10-15% service charge often included in bills. Additional tips appreciated for exceptional service.

Suggested 5-Day Dubai Itinerary

This is a suggested itinerary that can be fully customized based on your interests, duration, and budget. Dubai offers endless possibilities!

Day 1

Arrival & Dubai Marina

Welcome to Dubai! Upon arrival at Dubai International Airport, you'll be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel.

After checking in and freshening up, spend your first afternoon/evening exploring Dubai Marina:

  • Walk along the stunning Marina Walk - a 7km promenade with cafes, restaurants, and yacht views
  • Visit The Beach at JBR (Jumeirah Beach Residence) - beach clubs, restaurants, and shopping
  • Take photos at the Marina's impressive skyscrapers
  • Optional: Book a Dubai Marina Dhow Cruise (evening dinner cruise on traditional boat with buffet dinner and entertainment)

Enjoy dinner at one of Marina's many restaurants offering cuisines from around the world.

Accommodation: Selected hotel

Meals: Dinner (optional)

Day 2

Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall & Downtown Dubai

After breakfast, head to Downtown Dubai for a day of iconic experiences:

Morning/Afternoon - Burj Khalifa:

  • Ascend to the 124th & 125th floor observation decks (At The Top) of the world's tallest building
  • Enjoy breathtaking 360-degree views of Dubai's skyline, desert, and ocean
  • Interactive displays about Dubai's development and Burj Khalifa construction
  • Upgrade option: 148th floor SKY lounge for highest observation deck

Afternoon - Dubai Mall:

  • Explore the world's largest shopping mall with 1,200+ stores
  • Visit the Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo - walk through a 48-meter tunnel surrounded by sharks and rays
  • See the Dubai Fountain - world's largest choreographed fountain (shows every 30 minutes in evening)
  • Shop international brands, dine at dozens of restaurants, visit VR Park or Kidzania

Evening: Watch the spectacular Dubai Fountain show (6:00 PM onwards, every 30 minutes). The water, music, and light show is mesmerizing!

Dinner at Dubai Mall or nearby Souk Al Bahar with fountain views.

Accommodation: Selected hotel

Meals: Breakfast

Day 3

Desert Safari Adventure

Morning at leisure - relax by the hotel pool or explore nearby attractions.

Afternoon/Evening - Desert Safari Experience:

Around 3:00 PM, you'll be picked up from your hotel for an unforgettable Desert Safari Adventure:

  • Dune Bashing - Thrilling 4x4 ride over red sand dunes with an experienced driver (30-45 minutes of adrenaline!)
  • Camel Riding - Experience traditional desert transport
  • Sandboarding - Surf down the dunes
  • Sunset Photography - Capture stunning desert sunset views
  • Traditional Bedouin Camp - Arrive at an authentic desert camp

At the Desert Camp:

  • Henna painting for ladies
  • Shisha (water pipe) smoking experience
  • Try traditional Arabic coffee and dates
  • Watch Tanoura dance and belly dancing performances
  • Delicious BBQ buffet dinner with Arabic and international dishes
  • Grilled meats, salads, Arabic mezze, desserts
  • Vegetarian options available

Stargazing in the desert before returning to hotel around 9:30 PM.

Accommodation: Selected hotel

Meals: Breakfast, Desert BBQ Dinner

Day 4

Old Dubai Heritage & Palm Jumeirah

Morning - Old Dubai Heritage Tour:

Discover Dubai's rich history and traditions:

  • Al Fahidi Historical District - Wander through narrow lanes of restored traditional buildings, art galleries, and cafes
  • Dubai Museum - Located in Al Fahidi Fort (built 1787), learn about Dubai's transformation from fishing village to global metropolis
  • Gold Souk - Browse hundreds of shops displaying dazzling gold jewelry (Dubai is known as "City of Gold")
  • Spice Souk - Colorful market with aromatic spices, dried fruits, nuts, and traditional remedies
  • Abra Ride - Cross Dubai Creek on a traditional water taxi (just 1 AED!)

Afternoon - Palm Jumeirah:

  • Take the Palm Monorail for views of the iconic palm-shaped island
  • Visit The Pointe - waterfront dining and entertainment destination with Atlantis views
  • Photo stop at Atlantis The Palm resort
  • Drive along the Palm's crescent for stunning views
  • Optional: Visit Aquaventure Waterpark or Lost Chambers Aquarium at Atlantis

Evening:

  • Photo stop at the iconic Burj Al Arab (world's most luxurious hotel)
  • Visit Madinat Jumeirah - recreated traditional souk with boutiques, restaurants, and Burj Al Arab views
  • Optional: Dinner at one of Madinat's waterfront restaurants

Accommodation: Selected hotel

Meals: Breakfast

Day 5

Shopping & Leisure / Departure

Your final day in Dubai - morning at leisure based on your departure time.

Morning Shopping & Activities (if time permits):

  • Mall of the Emirates - Shop at 630+ stores, visit Ski Dubai (indoor ski slope in the desert!)
  • Ibn Battuta Mall - Themed mall celebrating the travels of Arab explorer Ibn Battuta
  • Global Village (seasonal Oct-Apr) - Cultural and entertainment destination with pavilions from 75+ countries
  • Dubai Frame - Architectural landmark offering old and new Dubai views

Optional Additional Activities:

  • IMG Worlds of Adventure - world's largest indoor theme park
  • Dubai Miracle Garden - world's largest flower garden (seasonal)
  • La Mer Beach - Trendy beach destination with street art and dining
  • Last-minute souvenir shopping at Dubai Mall

Check out from hotel and transfer to Dubai International Airport for your departure flight.

Leave with incredible memories of this futuristic city where anything is possible!

Meals: Breakfast

Transfers: Hotel to airport included
