Package Overview

Experience Bali's rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty on this educational journey. From traditional Balinese dance and cooking to volcanic landscapes and island adventures, students will immerse themselves in Indonesian culture while exploring tropical ecosystems and island geography.

📍 Destinations: Ubud, Kintamani, South Bali, Tabanan, Nusa Penida
🎓 Educational Focus: Cultural Studies, Ecology, Geography, Agriculture

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Bali – Ubud

Meals: None | Overnight: Ubud

Upon arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport in Bali, the group will be warmly welcomed with traditional Balinese flower garlands. They will then be transferred to Ubud, a cultural center of Bali, for hotel check-in. The rest of the day will be at leisure, giving everyone time to rest and acclimatize to the tropical surroundings.

Day 2: Kintamani & Ubud Exploration

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch | Overnight: Ubud

After breakfast, the day begins with a cultural visit to Peliatan Palace, where students will:

  • Dress in traditional Balinese attire
  • Learn the art of making offerings (Canang)
  • Experience the basics of Balinese dance

From there, the group will head to Kintamani for breathtaking views of Mount Batur and Lake Batur, learning about the geological significance of the volcano. A buffet lunch with panoramic views will be served before visiting Alas Harum, a one-stop destination featuring rice terraces, coffee plantations, and several iconic photo spots. The day concludes with a return to the hotel in Ubud.

Day 3: Trekking, Cooking & Temples

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch | Overnight: Ubud

The morning starts with trekking at Tjampuhan Hill, where participants will experience Ubud's lush natural landscapes. Afterwards, they will join a hands-on Balinese cooking class, learning to prepare authentic dishes that will also be enjoyed for lunch.

The afternoon is dedicated to visiting a traditional coffee plantation to understand the process of producing Balinese coffee and the world-famous Luwak coffee. The day ends at Tirta Empul, a sacred water temple where holy spring water is used in purification rituals, offering cultural and spiritual insight.

Day 4: Uluwatu Temple & Cultural Performances

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner | Overnight: South Bali

The group will enjoy a relaxed morning before checking out from their Ubud hotel and heading south. The first highlight is Uluwatu Temple, a Hindu temple dramatically perched on a cliff overlooking the Indian Ocean. Here, students will also encounter the playful monkeys that inhabit the area.

As evening approaches, they will watch the traditional Kecak Dance, which narrates the Ramayana epic in a mesmerizing performance. The day concludes with a seafood dinner on Jimbaran Beach before checking into their hotel in South Bali.

Day 5: Tabanan Highlands Tour

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch | Overnight: South Bali

After breakfast, the group departs for Tabanan, starting with Wanagiri Hidden Village, famous for its scenic photo spots with Buyan Lake as a backdrop. They then visit Ulun Danu Beratan Temple, an iconic Balinese water temple floating on Lake Beratan. Here, students will learn about the temple's importance in local Hindu traditions and its role in the island's irrigation system.

A buffet lunch follows before heading to a strawberry plantation, where participants can pick strawberries and enjoy fresh strawberry juice. In the evening, the group returns to their hotel.

Day 6: Nusa Penida Island Adventure

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch | Overnight: South Bali

An early departure takes the group to Benoa Harbour for a fast boat transfer to Nusa Penida Island. The day's tour includes visits to:

  • Broken Beach - Natural rock arch formation
  • Angel's Billabong - Natural infinity pool
  • Kelingking Cliff - Famous for its T-Rex/dinosaur-shaped formation
  • Crystal Bay - Beautiful beach ideal for relaxation

A buffet lunch will be provided on the island before the group returns by boat to Bali in the late afternoon. The evening is free at leisure at the hotel.

Day 7: Departure

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: N/A

After breakfast, there will be free time for relaxation or last-minute shopping before check-out. The group will then be transferred to the airport three hours before departure, marking the end of a memorable week of cultural discovery, adventure, and learning in Bali.
