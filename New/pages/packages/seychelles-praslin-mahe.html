6-Day Seychelles Praslin & Mahe Extravaganza

PRICE USD 1,745 per person
DURATION 6 Days / 5 Nights
TOUR TYPE Two-Island Escape
DESTINATIONS Praslin & Mahe

Holiday Overview

Experience the best of Seychelles with our 6-day two-island adventure combining the legendary beaches of Praslin with the vibrant culture of Mahe. This perfectly balanced itinerary allows you to discover two contrasting paradises in the Indian Ocean.

Begin your journey on Praslin Island, home to the UNESCO World Heritage Site Vallée de Mai and some of the world's most spectacular beaches including Anse Lazio and Anse Georgette. Then transfer to Mahe Island, the largest island in Seychelles, where you'll explore the charming capital Victoria, drive along scenic coastal roads, and relax on pristine beaches. This package combines natural wonders, cultural experiences, and plenty of time for relaxation.

Experience Highlights

  • Explore Vallée de Mai UNESCO World Heritage Site – home to the legendary Coco de Mer palm
  • Swim at Anse Lazio, consistently rated one of the world's most beautiful beaches
  • Visit Anse Georgette beach, a hidden gem accessible through the Constance Lemuria Resort
  • Take a scenic ferry ride between Praslin and Mahe through turquoise waters
  • Discover Victoria's colonial architecture and bustling Sir Selwyn Selwyn-Clarke Market
  • Explore Mahe's diverse beaches from dramatic surf at Anse Intendance to calm Beau Vallon
  • Experience authentic Creole cuisine blending African, French, Chinese, and Indian flavors
  • Snorkel in crystal-clear waters teeming with tropical fish and marine turtles

Important Notes

  • Climate: Seychelles enjoys a tropical climate. Best weather is April-May and October-November. Temperatures range from 24-32°C year-round.
  • Visa: All visitors receive a free visitor's permit on arrival valid for up to 3 months. No pre-arranged visa required.
  • Currency: Seychellois Rupee (SCR). US Dollars and Euros widely accepted. Credit cards accepted at hotels and restaurants.
  • Ferry Between Islands: Cat Cocos ferry operates daily between Mahe and Praslin (1 hour journey). Advance booking recommended during peak season.
  • Vallée de Mai: Entry fee approximately 350 SCR (about USD 25). Guided tours available. Wear comfortable walking shoes and bring water.
  • Language: Creole, English, and French are official languages. English widely spoken in tourist areas.
  • Health: No mandatory vaccinations. Bring reef-safe sunscreen, insect repellent, and stay hydrated. UV index is high year-round.
  • Beach Safety: Some beaches have strong currents during monsoon seasons. Always check local conditions and swim at beaches with lifeguards when possible.
  • Coco de Mer: The famous double coconut is protected. Purchase only from licensed vendors if you wish to take one home (export permit required).

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival Mahe, Transfer to Praslin

Welcome to Seychelles! Your tropical adventure begins with arrival at Seychelles International Airport on Mahe Island.

Upon clearing customs, you'll be met by our representative who will assist you with your domestic flight transfer to Praslin Island (approximately 15-minute scenic flight) or ferry transfer (1-hour journey through turquoise waters).

On arrival at Praslin, transfer to your hotel and check in. Praslin is the second-largest island in Seychelles, famous for its UNESCO World Heritage Site and spectacular beaches.

Depending on your arrival time, you might want to:

  • Take a refreshing swim at nearby Côte d'Or beach
  • Explore the immediate surroundings of your hotel
  • Enjoy a welcome drink at a beachfront bar
  • Watch your first Seychelles sunset from the beach

Rest well tonight – tomorrow you'll explore the legendary Vallée de Mai!

Accommodation: Selected hotel in Praslin

Meals: None (breakfast from Day 2)

Transfers: Airport to Praslin included (flight or ferry)

Day 2

Vallée de Mai & Anse Lazio

After breakfast, embark on a full day of exploration starting with the crown jewel of Praslin – Vallée de Mai Nature Reserve.

Morning – Vallée de Mai UNESCO World Heritage Site:

  • Explore this primeval palm forest, unchanged since prehistoric times
  • See the legendary Coco de Mer palm with its famous double coconut (largest seed in the plant kingdom)
  • Walk well-maintained trails through the forest (approximately 2-3 hours)
  • Spot rare wildlife including the Seychelles Black Parrot (endemic to Praslin)
  • Learn about the unique flora and fauna from informative signs or optional guided tour
  • Visit the information center with exhibits about the forest's ecology

Afternoon – Anse Lazio Beach:

After your forest adventure, head to Anse Lazio, consistently rated one of the world's top beaches. This picture-perfect beach features:

  • Powder-white sand framed by massive granite boulders
  • Crystal-clear turquoise waters ideal for swimming
  • Excellent snorkeling among coral reefs and tropical fish
  • Beachside restaurants for lunch (Bonbon Plume, Anse Lazio Restaurant)
  • Shaded areas under takamaka trees for relaxation

Spend the afternoon swimming, sunbathing, and capturing incredible photographs. Return to your hotel in the late afternoon.

Accommodation: Selected hotel in Praslin

Meals: Breakfast

Activities: Vallée de Mai visit (entry fee not included), beach time

Day 3

Praslin Island Exploration & Beach Day

Today is yours to explore more of Praslin's treasures at your own pace. After breakfast, consider these options:

Suggested Activities (optional, not included):

  • Anse Georgette Beach – One of Seychelles' hidden gems, accessible through Constance Lemuria Resort (advance permission required). Pristine, uncrowded, absolutely breathtaking
  • Island Hopping to La Digue – Take a short ferry to neighboring La Digue island (famous for Anse Source d'Argent beach). Explore by bicycle in this car-free paradise
  • Curieuse Island Excursion – Boat trip to this marine park to see giant tortoises in their natural habitat and mangrove forests
  • Beach Hopping – Explore Praslin's other beautiful beaches: Anse Volbert (Côte d'Or), Anse Boudin, Anse Consolation
  • Snorkeling Trip – Join a boat excursion to pristine snorkeling sites around Praslin and nearby islands
  • Fond Ferdinand Nature Reserve – Alternative forest walk with more challenging trails and fewer crowds than Vallée de Mai

Alternatively, simply relax at your hotel pool or favorite beach with a good book. Praslin's laid-back atmosphere is perfect for unwinding.

Evening at leisure – enjoy fresh seafood at one of Praslin's excellent restaurants.

Accommodation: Selected hotel in Praslin

Meals: Breakfast

Activities: Day at leisure (optional activities at own expense)

Day 4

Ferry to Mahe Island

After breakfast and hotel check-out, transfer to the jetty for your inter-island ferry to Mahe (approximately 1-hour scenic journey).

The ferry ride itself is an experience – enjoy views of granite islands rising from turquoise waters, and keep an eye out for flying fish and dolphins that sometimes accompany the boat.

Upon arrival at Mahe, you'll be met and transferred to your hotel. After checking in and freshening up, you have the rest of the day to explore Mahe at your own pace.

Afternoon suggestions:

  • Beau Vallon Beach – Mahe's most popular beach with calm waters, water sports, and beachfront restaurants. Perfect for an afternoon swim
  • Victoria City – Quick exploration of the capital if time permits. Visit the Clock Tower, browse local shops, or stop at the market
  • Hotel Facilities – Relax by the pool after your island transfer

In the evening, consider dining at one of Mahe's excellent restaurants. Your hotel can recommend nearby options for authentic Creole cuisine or international fare.

Accommodation: Selected hotel in Mahe

Meals: Breakfast

Transfers: Hotel to ferry jetty (Praslin), ferry to Mahe, jetty to hotel (Mahe) – all included

Day 5

Mahe Island Tour & Southern Beaches

After breakfast, enjoy a full day exploring Mahe's diverse attractions. Car rental recommended for maximum flexibility.

Suggested morning route:

  • Victoria City – Explore the world's smallest capital city:
    • Sir Selwyn Selwyn-Clarke Market – colorful market with fresh fish, fruits, spices, and local crafts
    • Clock Tower – iconic silver replica of London's Little Ben
    • Botanical Gardens – home to giant tortoises and tropical plants
    • Hindu Temple – vibrant Arul Mihu Navasakthi Vinayagar Temple
  • Mission Lodge Viewpoint – Panoramic views over western Mahe (where Queen Elizabeth II once picnicked!)

Afternoon coastal drive to southern beaches:

  • Takamaka Bay Distillery – Tour and tasting of Seychelles' famous rum
  • Anse Intendance – Dramatic beach with powerful waves and pristine white sand (swimming with caution due to currents)
  • Anse Takamaka – Palm-fringed beach perfect for swimming and sunbathing. Enjoy lunch at a beachside restaurant
  • Anse Royale – Calm, family-friendly beach on the east coast
  • Jardin du Roi – Historic spice garden with restaurant and stunning views

The southern coastal road offers breathtaking scenery around every bend. Stop frequently for photographs!

Accommodation: Selected hotel in Mahe

Meals: Breakfast

Activities: Self-guided island exploration (car rental recommended)

Day 6

Departure – Farewell to Paradise

Enjoy your final breakfast in Seychelles. Depending on your flight time, you may have a few hours for:

  • Last-minute souvenir shopping in Victoria (Codevar craft center, Camion Hall market)
  • Final swim at Beau Vallon or another favorite beach
  • Relaxation by the hotel pool
  • One more plate of Creole curry or fresh grilled fish

Check out from your hotel at the appropriate time. Our representative will transfer you to Seychelles International Airport for your departure flight.

Depart with incredible memories of two contrasting island paradises – the forest wonders and beaches of Praslin, and the vibrant culture and coastal beauty of Mahe.

Bon voyage and we hope to welcome you back to Seychelles soon!

Meals: Breakfast

Transfers: Hotel to airport included
