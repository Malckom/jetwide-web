CHRISTMAS COASTAL DEALS - Diani Beach

FROM KSh 28,750
DURATION 5 Nights
TRAVEL PERIOD 22 Dec - 3 Jan
DESTINATION Diani Beach, Kenya

Package Overview

Celebrate Christmas and New Year on Africa's best beach! Experience the magic of the festive season at Diani Beach with its pristine white sands, turquoise waters, and world-class resorts. This 5-night package offers exceptional value with 6 carefully selected properties, all featuring special Christmas and New Year's celebrations.

📍 Location: Diani Beach, South Coast
⏰ Travel Period: 22nd December - 3rd January
🚆 Transport: Return SGR + Ukunda transfers

Package Highlights

  • 5 nights accommodation at top Diani Beach resorts
  • Christmas Eve gala dinner and New Year's celebrations
  • Meals as per hotel meal plan (BB, Half Board, or All-Inclusive)
  • Return SGR train tickets from Nairobi to Mombasa
  • Comfortable transfers to Diani Beach via Likoni Ferry
  • Access to 25km of award-winning white sandy beach
  • Festive entertainment, themed dining, and special activities

Why Celebrate at Diani Beach?

  • Africa's Best Beach: Consistently ranked among the world's top 10 beaches
  • All-Inclusive Paradise: Multiple AI resorts with everything included
  • Family-Friendly: Excellent facilities and programs for all ages
  • Amazing Value: Starting from just KSh 28,750 for 5 festive nights
  • Perfect Weather: Guaranteed sunshine during the Christmas season

Festive Season Celebrations

  • Christmas Eve gala buffets with live entertainment
  • New Year's Eve beach parties and fireworks displays
  • Special Christmas Day lunch with traditional festivities
  • Live music, DJs, and cultural performances
  • Kids' programs with Santa visits and activities
  • Themed dining experiences throughout the season
  • Water sports and beach activities
  • Kite surfing, diving, and snorkeling adventures
  • Shimba Hills safari day trips

Important Notes

  • Minimum stay: 5 nights during peak festive season
  • Full payment required 14 days before travel
  • Festive supplements included in the quoted rates
