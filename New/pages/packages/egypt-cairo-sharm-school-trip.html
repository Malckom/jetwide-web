Package Overview

From the timeless magic of the Pyramids and Sphinx to the sun-drenched shores of Sharm El Sheikh, this educational journey blends ancient wonders with Red Sea relaxation. Students will dive into Egypt's rich history in Cairo, then unwind by the beach, snorkel vibrant reefs, or simply soak in the charm of the coast.

📍 Destination: Cairo & Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt
✈️ Route: Nairobi → Cairo (Direct Flight)
🎓 Educational Focus: Ancient Egyptian History, Marine Biology, Geology

Trip Highlights

  • Pyramids of Giza - Cheops, Chephren, and Menkaure
  • The Great Sphinx - Ancient guardian of the pyramids
  • Egyptian Museum - King Tutankhamun's treasures and mummies
  • Sharm El Sheikh - Red Sea resort relaxation
  • Ras Mohamed National Park - World-class snorkeling and diving
  • Optional desert adventures and Bedouin experiences

Educational Value

  • Ancient Egyptian civilization and architecture
  • Hieroglyphics and ancient writing systems
  • Mummification and burial practices
  • Marine ecosystems and coral reef conservation
  • Desert ecology and Bedouin culture

Learning Outcomes

Students will gain firsthand knowledge of one of the world's oldest civilizations, understand the engineering marvels of the ancient world, and explore marine biodiversity in the Red Sea. This trip links curriculum topics in history, science, geography, and cultural studies.

Safety & Supervision

Professional tour guides accompany the group throughout. All hotels and transportation are carefully selected for student safety and comfort. Activities are age-appropriate and supervised by experienced staff.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival in Cairo

Meals: None | Overnight: Cairo Hotel

Arrive at Cairo International Airport where our representative will be waiting to greet and assist you through immigration and customs. You'll then be transferred to your hotel in a private air-conditioned vehicle. Check-in and enjoy your first night in Cairo. Evening briefing about the exciting days ahead.

Day 02: Cairo Sightseeing

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Cairo Hotel

After breakfast, embark on a fascinating journey to the Giza Plateau. Stand in awe before the legendary Pyramids of Kings Cheops, Chephren, and Menkaure. These monumental structures are among the most recognizable landmarks on Earth and stand as lasting symbols of ancient Egyptian civilization.

Guarding them is the enigmatic Sphinx, carved from limestone with the body of a lion and the head of a pharaoh, representing strength and wisdom. Students will learn about ancient engineering techniques and the pharaohs' beliefs about the afterlife.

Continue your tour at the Egyptian Museum in Tahrir Square. This world-renowned museum houses more than 120,000 ancient artifacts including mummies, jewelry, statues, and the dazzling golden collection of King Tutankhamun. Return to your hotel for an overnight in Cairo.

Day 03: Drive to Sharm El Sheikh

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Sharm El Sheikh

After breakfast and check-out, begin your journey to Sharm El Sheikh, located on the southern tip of the Sinai Peninsula. This renowned resort town is a paradise between desert and sea, known for its crystal-clear waters, sandy beaches, and vibrant coral reefs.

The drive takes approximately 6-7 hours through the Sinai Desert, offering students a chance to observe desert landscapes and learn about desert ecology. Upon arrival, check in to your all-inclusive resort. You may explore the beach, unwind by the pool, or enjoy optional excursions. Overnight in Sharm El Sheikh.

Day 04: Sharm El Sheikh at Leisure

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner (All-Inclusive) | Overnight: Sharm El Sheikh

Enjoy a full day of leisure at your beach resort. You can relax under the sun, swim in the warm waters of the Red Sea, or join one of the many optional excursions.

Educational Activities Include:

  • Snorkeling at Ras Mohamed National Park - One of Egypt's top diving destinations featuring colorful reefs and abundant marine life. Students learn about coral reef ecosystems and marine conservation.
  • Glass-bottom boat tours - View coral reefs and tropical fish without getting wet
  • Beach ecology studies - Observe coastal ecosystems and marine environments

Day 05: Sharm El Sheikh at Leisure

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner (All-Inclusive) | Overnight: Sharm El Sheikh

Another day to enjoy the beauty of Sharm El Sheikh. Whether you prefer water sports, desert adventures, or simply lounging by the sea, the day is yours to explore or unwind.

Optional Activities Include:

  • Quad biking in the desert - Experience the Sinai desert landscape
  • Camel rides - Traditional Bedouin mode of transport
  • Boat trips - Explore nearby islands and snorkeling spots
  • Bedouin dinner experience - Learn about traditional Bedouin culture, food, and customs under the stars
  • Desert safari - Visit a Bedouin village and learn about desert survival

Day 06: Return to Cairo

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner | Overnight: Cairo Hotel

After breakfast and check-out, begin the journey back to Cairo by private vehicle (approximately 6–7 hours' drive). Students can reflect on their experiences and share what they've learned about marine biology and coastal ecosystems.

Upon arrival, check in to your hotel and enjoy your final evening in the capital. Optional evening activity: visit Khan el-Khalili bazaar for souvenir shopping and experience traditional Egyptian market culture.

Day 07: Departure

Meals: Breakfast | Overnight: N/A

Enjoy your final breakfast in Egypt. After check-out, transfer to Cairo International Airport for your departure flight. Students depart with lasting memories of pyramids, beaches, and the warm Egyptian hospitality, along with valuable educational insights into ancient civilizations and marine ecosystems.
