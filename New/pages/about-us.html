About Us

JETWIDE CONSORTIUM

Our Story

Jetwide Consortium Limited is a licensed and accredited private employment and travel agency, proudly connecting Kenyan nationals with life-changing opportunities both locally and abroad. We specialize in professional and domestic worker placements in Saudi Arabia, the UAE, and Qatar, strictly adhering to the Labour Institutions Act, 2007 and all Ministry of Labour regulations.

Beyond recruitment, Jetwide stands out as a trusted partner in visa facilitation, student placement, and travel management — ensuring every journey, whether for work, study, or leisure, is seamless and compliant. Our credibility is built on full certification from the Ministry of Labour, the Chamber of Commerce, and the respective embassies of the countries we represent.

We believe in partnerships founded on trust, integrity, and service excellence. That's why both clients and candidates rely on us for transparent processes, timely delivery, and dedicated support every step of the way.

Contact Us

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, ethical, and fully compliant recruitment and travel solutions that connect talent to opportunity while upholding the highest standards of customer service and professionalism.

We work hand-in-hand with employers, institutions, and government bodies to ensure that every placement and service reflects Jetwide's promise of quality, integrity, and total client satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred recruitment and travel agency in Kenya — empowering people to explore, work, and thrive across borders while maintaining our reputation for excellence, integrity, and care.

Our Compass:
People & Places

At Jetwide, we believe travel is about connection. It's about the people you meet, the cultures you encounter, and the places that leave a lasting mark on your heart. Our compass doesn't just point to destinations on a map — it points to experiences that matter.

We celebrate people by creating opportunities: helping clients take their first safari, guiding professionals into international careers, and supporting travelers as they discover new horizons. We value places by working with local guides and partners who share our respect for nature, culture, and community. Integrity and trust shape every decision, because your journey deserves nothing less.
